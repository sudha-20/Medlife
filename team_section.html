MEET OUR TEAM

TEAM - “Together Everyone Achieves More”

Medlife Foundation is an Innovative, Disciplined, Professional and a fully focused Healthcare Organization in India. It is the people who make, build, develop and drive the organizations. Our dedicated team at Medlife Foundation is Innovative, Disciplined and shows professionalism in every act to serve the community at large, the action of every member in our team makes Medlife Foundation an Innovative and a Disciplined Organization in the Eyes of the Community to whom we serve every day.All people should be treated with equal importance without any segregation or consolidation of power and money.


Bhushan Shirude

CEO & Founder

Bhushan is an Entrepreneur, Business Consultant based in India. He has a Broad Vision to provide opportunities to Youth and empower them to become the Future of the Developing India. Medlife Foundation does not want to grow only as an organization but wants to grow as an Ideal of a Sustainable Future.... Disciplined people, social changes with innovation, connecting youth, humanity and equality where organizations and individuals across the world take up the lead work, learning from our experience and help reach the basics of life to people who need it urgently, mindful of their dignity and their needs, not as a charity. We provide youth, an opportunity to be the change and make a difference. We may not be able to perfect the world in our lifetime, but every effort we put forth counts. Bhushan has received Management degree (MBA) from SIES College of Management Studies & has Experience of Corporate World for more than 4 years in the Pharmaceutical Industry (Sales & Marketing). After which he left the job and founded Medlife foundation for full-time social work to empower people. He is the very passionate about Social Entrepreneurship and has a good business acumen and has been continuously striving for something innovative. Apart from this, Bhushan is a foodie who loves photography, travel, reading, writing and has a vision of conquering the world.

Sandeep Shirude

President

Sandeep is a multifaceted and multitalented young individual and a Social Entrepreneur, who mentors the organization in its efforts to Sustainable Growth. He holds a Strong Background in handling Initiatives and his working strongly in the social sector for creating a better society.... He has been very passionate about Social work and founded Medlife Foundation in 2016 with a mission to bring Positive Social Changes and deliver value to the society by Improving the Quality of life of Poor people for the Future. We understand the problems in the society and wish to play a pivotal role in making the difference. He has set out to play a vital role in growing the organization as well as society. He has done his pharmacy at North Maharashtra University. His hobbies include reading, traveling and socializing and networking.
