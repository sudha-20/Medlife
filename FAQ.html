Medlife Foundation was established in the year 2014

An NGO Registered under The Bombay Trusts Act,1950, it’s a Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) was established on 22nd January 2014 by Bhushan Shirude and Sandeep Shirude

Medlife Foundation has been working in the areas of education, healthcare, child development ,sports for development,Women empowerment,agricultural transformation,and social entrepreneurship program.

Medlife Foundation works under various models,like Healthcare awareness campaign,education Seminars,Agricultural exhibition, sports awareness ,and social entrepreneurship program for rural people and so on.

Around 70-80% funding support of the organization comes from corporates, individual donors, and private institutions.

Medlife Foundation looks for emerging and committed grassroots organizations for reaching out to underprivileged communities. We partner with people and organizations that want to make a difference but do not have adequate knowledge and capacity to raise resources or work in a professional manner.
